My essential question I will be addressing is, How does nature affect humans? In order to begin this project I must ask myself why I’m interested in this question. Nature surrounds me and plays a huge role in my life and always will. Nature seems to have a calming effect on humans in general but I would like to explore deeper into nature. It is in my interest to find how nature comes into play in my life and how I could perhaps incorporate it more. It is easier to ask myself how this question could not be essential to someone’s life. In order to address my question, I will spend time documenting prominent natural phenomenons that affect me most. Primarily I will be photographing waves since they play the biggest role in my life. Other landscapes and natural occurrences often spur my curiosity as well. The reason I will be using photography is because it gives me the ability to capture a moment in time and provides me with more than just a memory to study and examine that moment into fine detail. I will also be using poetry as a way to analyze the photos I take. I feel that poetry forces the writer to look in fine detail for the things that count. Poetry is usually shorter so it requires a lot of mental strength and focus to find one word to describe multiple things occurring in the picture.
